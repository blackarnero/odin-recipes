Pumpkin Spice Latte

from pickuplimes.com

Description

This pumpkin spice latte recipe will blow your local coffee shop's version out of the water. Why go out and buy one when you can make a superior version on your own? After this, you will be feeling like a posh barista.

Ingredients

Pumpkin spice mix

  • 1/3 cup (42g) ground cinnamon
  • 1 & 1/2 tbsp (8g) ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp (7g) ground nutmeg
  • 2 tsp (4g) ground allspice
  • 2 tsp (4.5g) ground cloves

Pumpkin spice latte

  • 2 cups (480ml) barista oat milk
  • 2 tbsp (31g) canned pumpkin puree
  • 2 tbsp (25g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin spice mix(see above)
  • 2/3 cup (160ml) brewed espresso
  • 1 tsp (5ml) pure vanilla extract

Directions/Steps

  1. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, combine the milk, pumpkin puree, sugar, and pumpkin spice mix. Stir until heated through.
  2. Pour the espresso into your desired cups.
  3. Stir vanilla into the milk and divide between your cups.
  4. Top with some foamed milk or whipped cream if desired and a sprinkle of cinnamon. Enjoy!
