Brownies

Description

This brownies recipe will make the god of flavor come down from flavor heaven and shake you mortal hands. Scroll down to ascend your brownies-making skills to godhood.

Ingredients

Brownies

  • 1/4 cup (60ml) brewed espresso
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) water
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup (75g) light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp (10ml) vanilla extract
  • 7.1 oz (200g) dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 1 & 1/2 cups (205g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (48g) dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp (4g) baking powder

Finishing touches

  • 2.1 oz (60g) dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 pinch flaky sea salt

Directions/Steps

  1. Add the espresso and water to a large bowl, mix and set aside.
  2. Line an 8-inch light metal square metal cake pan with parchment paper and preheat the oven to 350F/180C
  3. Returning to the large bowl, add the granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Top with some foamed milk or whipped cream if desired and a sprinkle of cinnamon. Enjoy!
